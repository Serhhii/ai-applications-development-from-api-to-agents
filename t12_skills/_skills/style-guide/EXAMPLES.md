# Style Guide Examples

## Email Subject Lines

| Before ❌ | After ✅ | Rule Applied |
|---|---|---|
| "Important Update Regarding Your Account" | "Your account has been updated" | Sentence case, cut filler |
| "We Are Pleased to Inform You of a New Feature" | "New: [Feature name] is live" | Lead with the news |
| "Please Read: Action Required" | "Action needed: confirm your email" | Specific, direct |
| "Monthly Newsletter – Issue 12" | "5 things we shipped this month" | Benefit-led, curiosity |

---

## Email Body Rewrites

**Before ❌**
> Dear valued customer,
> We are pleased to inform you that your request has been processed successfully by our dedicated support team. Should you require any further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us at your earliest convenience.
> Kind regards,
> The Support Team

**After ✅**
> Hi there,
> Great news — your request has been processed. You're all set.
> Need anything else? Just reply to this email.
> Thanks,
> Support

**Changes:** Removed "valued customer" (cliché), cut passive voice, replaced formal sign-off, shortened by 40%.

## Common Jargon → Plain English

| Jargon ❌ | Plain English ✅ |
|---|---|
| Leverage | Use |
| Synergy | Working together |
| Going forward | From now on |
| Circle back | Follow up |
| Deep dive | Look closely |
| Pain point | Problem |
| Bandwidth | Capacity / time |
| Low-hanging fruit | Easy wins |

## Social Media Rewrites

**LinkedIn — Before ❌**
> We are excited to announce that we have launched our new platform! This innovative solution will leverage cutting-edge AI to synergize your workflow and help you achieve your business goals. #Innovation #AI #BusinessGrowth #Tech #FutureOfWork

**LinkedIn — After ✅**
> We just launched something we've been building for months.
>
> It uses AI to cut the repetitive parts of your workflow — so you can focus on the work that actually matters.
>
> Try it free for 30 days → [link]
>
> #AI #Productivity

**Changes:** Removed hype words, cut to 3 hashtags, added a clear CTA, opened with a human hook.

---

**Twitter/X — Before ❌**
> Thrilled to share that our Q3 results exceeded expectations! We achieved significant growth across all key performance indicators. #Success

**Twitter/X — After ✅**
> Q3 results: revenue up 34%, users up 2×. Here's what drove it → [link]

**Changes:** Led with numbers, cut buzzwords, stayed under 140 chars.

## Support Reply Rewrites

**Before ❌**
> Dear user, we have received your support ticket and it is currently being processed by our technical team. We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused. Please be advised that resolution times may vary depending on ticket volume.

**After ✅**
> Hey — thanks for reaching out.
>
> I've picked up your ticket and I'm looking into it now. I'll have an update for you within two hours.
>
> Sorry for the trouble. Let's get this sorted.

**Changes:** Dropped the formal opener, made it personal ("I"), gave a concrete time commitment, empathy-first tone.
